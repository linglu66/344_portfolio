The Story

All we knew is that we wanted to use GANs to make some cool sh*t. Tranforming people's facial expressions hadn't been done yet, so why couldn't we be the first? We narrowed down the exact problem we wanted to solve to - given a person's neutral face, can we make a model to generate that person's face happy, surprised and sad?
